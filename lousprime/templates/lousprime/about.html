About Us

Lou's List Prime began as a CS3240 class project to build a successor to the original Lou's List as its creator, Lou Bloomfield, entered retirement. However, it quickly became clear that the project had the potential to be much more expansive than the original Lou's List. Our team of five students, all of whom are Computer Science majors at the University of Virginia, have worked to take the best parts of Lou's List and expand upon them to streamline the course selection process for UVA students. Lou's List Prime now boasts a more intuitive interface, a robust schedule building system, and a social network that allows students to connect with each other through their coursework.

We welcome your feedback. If you have any questions or concerns please do not hesitate to contact us.
